This demo walks you through registering a new validator on your Permissionless L1 by staking native tokens. The tool handles all three phases:

1. **Initiate** - Lock your native tokens and start the registration
2. **P-Chain** - Sign and submit the registration to the P-Chain
3. **Complete** - Finalize the registration to activate your validator

Open the unified [**Add Validator** flow in the Builder Console](/console/add-validator/select-subnet) and pick your Native PoS L1 — the tool will auto-detect that it's backed by a Native Staking Manager and switch into the native-staking variant of these steps.

You can view the changes applied to the validator set by [re-running the query in section 2](/academy/permissionless-l1s/06-staking-manager-operations/02-query-pos-validator-set).
