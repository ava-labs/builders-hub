## Overview

[ShapeShift](https://shapeshift.com) is an **open-source, self-custodial multichain DeFi platform**: a single gateway to swap, bridge, stake, and earn across 48+ chains, including **Avalanche C-Chain**. It combines a non-custodial wallet with a DEX and bridge aggregator, so users reach the whole DeFi ecosystem from one interface while keeping full custody of their keys. No sign-up, no KYC, no custody of user funds. The protocol is owned and governed by the ShapeShift DAO.

Rather than sourcing liquidity from a single venue, ShapeShift aggregates routes across multiple DEXs and cross-chain protocols and surfaces the best-priced execution for every trade. Users move seamlessly between chains and trade 10,000+ assets from one place.

## Features

- **Self-Custodial Multichain Wallet**: Manage assets across 48+ chains, including Avalanche C-Chain (chainId 43114), with full custody and no KYC.
- **Best-Price DEX Aggregation**: Trades routed across aggregated liquidity for optimal execution.
- **Cross-Chain Swaps & Bridging**: Move assets natively between chains in one flow, no manual bridging.
- **Stake & Earn**: Put assets to work with one-click DeFi yield across the ecosystem.
- **Fiat On/Off Ramps**: Buy and sell crypto directly with fiat.
- **Hardware Wallet Support**: Connect Ledger or KeepKey for permissionless, private self-custody.
- **Open Source & DAO-Governed**: Fully open-source codebase, community-owned, free to use and build on.

## Getting Started

1. **Use the app**: Open [app.shapeshift.com](https://app.shapeshift.com), connect a self-custodial wallet (browser, Ledger, or KeepKey), and start swapping, bridging, staking, or earning across chains.
2. **Build with ShapeShift**: Embed ShapeShift's multichain swaps and cross-chain routing into your own dApp with the ShapeShift [widget](https://widget.shapeshift.com) and [API](https://api.shapeshift.com/).
