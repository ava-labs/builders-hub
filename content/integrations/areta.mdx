## Overview

Areta is a security marketplace built for Avalanche. Instead of emailing audit firms
one at a time and waiting on each to scope your code, you describe your project once and
let vetted auditors compete for the work. Quotes come back so you can compare price,
timeline, and fit before you commit.

The marketplace is free for the teams requesting reviews, and it stays free. Areta earns
on the auditor side, so there is no platform fee for builders.

It is also the marketplace that several firms in this directory already point to. If you
open the [OpenZeppelin](/integrations/openzeppelin-audit) or [BailSec](/integrations/bailsec)
pages here, their "Access Through Areta Marketplace" links send you to the same place.

## How it works

1. **Submit your scope.** Share the repository, the contracts in scope, and your target
   timeline through a guided form.
2. **Receive competing quotes.** Vetted firms respond with pricing and availability,
   usually within about 48 hours.
3. **Compare and choose.** Line up the offers, ask questions, and pick the firm that fits
   your budget and schedule.
4. **Engage directly.** Once you choose, you work with the auditor directly through the
   rest of the review.

## Why builders use it

- One request reaches more than 20 vetted firms instead of a handful of inboxes.
- Competing quotes tend to pull the price down rather than up.
- Pricing and terms are visible up front, so you compare like for like.
- It is free for the team requesting the audit.

## Audit subsidies

Eligible Avalanche projects can qualify for audit cashback through the marketplace, up to
$10k back on the cost of a review. Terms depend on the project and the program running at
the time, so check the current details when you submit.

## Auditors on the marketplace

Areta lists more than 20 security firms for Avalanche. Several of them have their own pages
in this directory, including [OpenZeppelin](/integrations/openzeppelin-audit) and
[BailSec](/integrations/bailsec), and you can reach them and the rest of the roster through
a single request rather than contacting each one separately.

## Getting started

1. Go to [areta.market/avalanche](https://areta.market/avalanche).
2. Submit your audit request with the scope and timeline.
3. Compare the quotes that come back.
4. Choose a firm and start the review.
