## Overview

Juno (a Bitso subsidiary) is a stablecoin issuer focused on Latin American markets, providing MXNB (Mexican Peso stablecoin) backed 1:1 by fiat reserves. Juno enables businesses and developers to integrate stablecoin payments, cross-border transactions, and fiat on/off-ramps for the Mexican and Latin American markets.
