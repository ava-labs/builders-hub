## Summary & Next Steps

To become an **Advanced Technical Team1 member**, you should:

- Already be comfortable with:
  - Blockchain & Avalanche fundamentals.
  - Solidity, ERC standards, frontend integration, and custom L1s (from the Technical Team1 course).[`https://build.avax.network/academy`](https://build.avax.network/academy)
- Build additional depth in:
  - **Interoperability**: ICM, ICTT, and bridging patterns on Avalanche.
  - **Encrypted ERC**: privacy‑preserving tokens and cryptographic underpinnings.
  - **x402**: HTTP‑native payments for web and AI experiences on Avalanche.

Use this course as a **skills map** and pair it with:

- Builder Hub Academy courses on Interchain Messaging, bridges, Encrypted ERC, and x402.[`https://build.avax.network/academy`](https://build.avax.network/academy)
- Team1 bootcamps and workshops (like the Latam Bootcamp) that turn these concepts into real projects and hackathon‑ready prototypes.[`https://team1-latam-bootcamp.gitbook.io/team1-docs`](https://team1-latam-bootcamp.gitbook.io/team1-docs)

As you master these topics, you’ll be able to design and support advanced Avalanche applications, mentor other Team1 members, and lead deep‑dive technical workshops in your region.
