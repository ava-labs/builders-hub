## Overview

Rise & Shine Management offers comprehensive Blockchain as a Service (BaaS) solutions for Avalanche, providing dedicated RPC services and Layer 1 infrastructure to support blockchain developers and applications. Hosted on Microsoft Azure, Rise & Shine's BaaS platform ensures low-latency, high-throughput, and uninterrupted access to Avalanche networks, enabling organizations to focus on building innovative blockchain applications without managing complex infrastructure.

## Features

- **Dedicated RPC Service**: Low-latency, high-throughput, and uninterrupted access to the Avalanche C-Chain
- **Microsoft Azure Hosting**: Enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure hosted on Microsoft Azure for reliability and scalability
- **Layer 1 Infrastructure**: Comprehensive infrastructure support for Avalanche L1 Blockchain Applications
- **High Availability**: Uninterrupted service with enterprise-grade uptime and reliability
- **Performance Optimized**: Optimized for low-latency and high-throughput blockchain operations
- **Developer-Focused**: Streamlined access to Avalanche networks for application development and deployment
- **Enterprise Support**: Professional support for organizations building on Avalanche
