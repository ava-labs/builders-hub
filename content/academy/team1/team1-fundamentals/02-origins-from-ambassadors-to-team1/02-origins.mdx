## From Avalanche Ambassador DAO to Team1

### The Avalanche Ambassador DAO

In early 2024, the Avalanche Foundation launched the Avalanche Ambassador DAO as a global initiative to expand the Avalanche ecosystem through community participation.

The idea behind the program was simple: empower motivated community members around the world to help grow Avalanche locally. Instead of relying only on centralized teams, the Foundation created a structure where contributors could organize events, educate developers, and build regional communities.

Ambassadors participated in a wide range of activities, including:

- Organizing **meetups and workshops**
- Supporting **hackathons and developer events**
- Creating **educational content**
- Building relationships with **universities and local tech communities**
- Helping onboard **new developers and users**

This program quickly became one of the main grassroots initiatives in the ecosystem. Contributors from dozens of countries organized events, created learning materials, and introduced Avalanche to new audiences.

The Ambassador DAO demonstrated that **community-driven growth could scale globally**, especially in a decentralized ecosystem where builders and users are distributed around the world.

---

### Why the Program Evolved

As the community grew, it became clear that the traditional **“ambassador” model** did not fully capture the kind of participation that was emerging.

Many contributors were not only promoting Avalanche, they were:

- building tools  
- launching projects  
- organizing developer programs  
- producing technical content  
- supporting local builder ecosystems  

In other words, they were acting less like ambassadors and more like **active contributors to the ecosystem**.

To better reflect this broader role, the program evolved beyond the ambassador framework.

---

### The Creation of Team1

In 2025, the Ambassador DAO was restructured and rebranded as **Team1**.

Team1 represents a shift from a traditional ambassador program toward a **global contributor network** designed to support builders, creators, educators, and community leaders.

The new structure focuses on:

- **active ecosystem participation**
- **builder-driven initiatives**
- **local community leadership**
- **collaboration between contributors worldwide**

Instead of emphasizing advocacy alone, Team1 encourages members to contribute through tangible actions such as organizing events, supporting developers, creating educational resources, and helping new projects connect with the Avalanche ecosystem.
