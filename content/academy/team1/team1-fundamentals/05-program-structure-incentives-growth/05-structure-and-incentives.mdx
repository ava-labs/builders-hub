### Bounties, XP & Recognition

We have **bounty systems** and **recognition mechanisms** that:

- Reward members for concrete contributions like event organization, content creation, or ecosystem support.
- Track activity over time so consistent contributors can be recognized and often given more responsibility.

These systems are intended to:

- Encourage **high-quality contributions**.
- Make it easier to see who is active and reliable.
- Provide a layer of **fairness and transparency** to how opportunities are distributed.

### Example Growth Journeys

We know starting might be difficult and that's why we recommend first to just talk with active members of the community, learn from them and try to device a plan for growing. You then are going to be ready and excited to take on new challenges.

Common growth paths include:

- Starting by **supporting existing events**, then designing and leading your own.
- Beginning as a **content contributor**, then evolving into a go-to educator or workshop facilitator.
- Assisting local builders informally, then becoming a **builder advocate** or regional lead.

These journeys are not rigid tracks but examples of how members can progress within Team1. The thing we want most is for you to start and feel part of Team1.
