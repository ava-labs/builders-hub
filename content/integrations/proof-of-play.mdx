## Overview

Proof of Play vRNG (Verified Random Number Generator) is an optimized, secure, onchain random number generation solution tested at scale with over 485 million transactions. It is faster, more reliable, and more affordable than traditional VRF solutions. Developers can use it to build decentralized applications and games that need verifiable randomness -- from blockchain games and NFTs to random assignment systems and consensus mechanisms. Proof of Play offers engineering support and straightforward integration.
