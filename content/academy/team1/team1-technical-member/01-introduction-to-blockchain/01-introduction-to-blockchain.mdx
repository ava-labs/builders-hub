## Section 1 – Introduction to Blockchain

Every Team1 member should understand the basic concepts behind blockchain technology.  
The **Blockchain Fundamentals** track on Builder Hub is designed to teach these fundamentals with Avalanche‑aligned examples.[`https://build.avax.network/academy/blockchain`](https://build.avax.network/academy/blockchain)

**By the end of this section you should be able to:**

- **Explain what a blockchain is**
  - Describe a blockchain as a distributed, append‑only ledger secured by cryptography and consensus.
  - Relate it to real‑world use cases like payments, NFTs, DeFi, gaming, and identity.[`https://build.avax.network/academy/blockchain`](https://build.avax.network/academy/blockchain)

- **Explain why blockchain was created**
  - Connect concepts like double‑spend problems, trust minimization, and censorship resistance to the creation of Bitcoin and later networks.

- **Describe what a consensus mechanism is and name the most common ones**
  - Explain consensus as the process by which distributed nodes agree on the state of the ledger.
  - Identify and briefly describe well‑known mechanisms like Proof of Work, Proof of Stake, and Avalanche consensus.[`https://build.avax.network/academy/blockchain`](https://build.avax.network/academy/blockchain)

- **Differentiate public vs. private blockchains**
  - Explain who can read, write, and validate in each model, and give examples.

- **Explain keys, addresses, and wallets**
  - Difference between **private key**, **public key**, **compressed public key**, and **address**.
  - What a **wallet** is and how it manages keys and signs transactions.

- **Describe how blocks and mempools work**
  - Structure of a block (header, transactions, metadata).
  - What the **mempool** is and how transactions flow from users to inclusion in a block.

**Suggested learning path**

- Complete **Blockchain Fundamentals** in the Academy.[`https://build.avax.network/academy/blockchain`](https://build.avax.network/academy/blockchain)
- If available, follow a Team1 workshop or bootcamp module that covers blockchain basics, such as the initial sessions of the Latam Bootcamp by David/Freddy.[`https://team1-latam-bootcamp.gitbook.io/team1-docs`](https://team1-latam-bootcamp.gitbook.io/team1-docs)
