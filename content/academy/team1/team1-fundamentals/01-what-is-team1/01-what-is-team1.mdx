## What Is Team1?

### Official Definition and Mission

Team1 is described as:

> “a new global network of builders, developers, creatives, gamers and community members who grow Avalanche.”  
> — Avalanche Team1 page[`https://www.team1.network/`](https://www.team1.network/)

This program is designed to organically expand and elevate the global Avalanche community. The focus is not just on promotion, but on **sustainable, community-led growth** of the ecosystem.

The vision of Team1 is to empower and elevate community members to help build and run one of the biggest community-driven programs in Web3.

Putting these together, we can summarize the **mission of Team1** as:

- Empowering community members around the world to **grow Avalanche**, organizing and supporting workshops, hackathons, meetups, game nights, and other local events to bring people into the ecosystem.
- Supporting **builders, developers, students, creators, gamers, and organizers**, connecting talent with opportunities and answers.
- Doing this in a way that is **decentralized, scalable, and community-driven**, not just top-down marketing.

### Team1’s Role in the Avalanche Ecosystem

Team1 works as a **bridge between the Avalanche Foundation, builders, and local communities**.

While Ava Labs focuses on developing the core technology of Avalanche and the Avalanche Foundation supports ecosystem growth through funding and programs, Team1 operates at the community layer of the ecosystem.

Through meetups, hackathons, university partnerships, and educational initiatives, Team1 helps onboard new builders and connect local communities to the global Avalanche network.

In this way, Team1 acts as a **grassroots engine for ecosystem growth**, ensuring that developers, students, and creators around the world can participate in building the next generation of decentralized applications.
