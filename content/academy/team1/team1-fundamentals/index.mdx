## Welcome to Team1 Fundamentals

The goal of this course is to give you a clear understanding of:

- **What Team1 is and why it exists**
- **Where it comes from**
- **What is expected from a Team1 member**
- **How Team1 members should interact with each other and with the wider ecosystem**

---

## Prerequisites

You **don’t** need to be a blockchain expert or a long-time Avalanche user to take this course.

However, to get the most out of it, we recommend:

- Having a basic understanding of **what Avalanche is** as a blockchain platform (for example by going through the Avalanche Fundamentals course).[`https://build.avax.network/academy/avalanche-l1/avalanche-fundamentals`](https://build.avax.network/academy/avalanche-l1/avalanche-fundamentals)
- Being interested in **community building, education, events, or helping builders**.

---

## Course Modules

This course is structured into five focused modules plus:

1. **Module 1: What Is Team1?**  
   Mission, official definitions, and Team1’s role in the Avalanche ecosystem.
2. **Module 2: Origins: From Avalanche Ambassadors to Team1**  
   How the Ambassador DAO evolved into today’s Team1 program.
3. **Module 3: What’s Expected from a Team1 Member?**  
   Values, contribution types, and expectations for members.
4. **Module 4: How Team1 Members Should Interact**  
   Channels, guidelines, and collaboration practices.
5. **Module 5: Program Structure, Incentives & Growth Paths**  
   How Team1 is organized, how incentives work, and example growth journeys.

Use the sidebar to navigate through each module, then finish by testing your knowledge with our quizzes.
