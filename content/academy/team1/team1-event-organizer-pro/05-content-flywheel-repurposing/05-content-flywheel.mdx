## Content flywheel and repurposing

IRL and online events are expensive attention machines. **Repurposing** is how you amortize that cost: one good night becomes proof for partners, onboarding for the next meetup, and a gentle path into Avalanche learning tracks.

Web3 teams often combine **short-form clips**, **written recaps**, and **quests or RSVPs** on aggregators (Luma-style pages, community platforms) so people can discover the same story in the channel they actually use. You do not need every channel. You need a **default stack** you can run while tired.

### The 48-hour capture window

Schedule three blocks on your calendar before the event happens:

| Time | Output | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- |
| T + 0 to 6 h | Raw clips folder + three best photos picked | Content capture role |
| T + 6 to 24 h | Short recap post (facts, thanks, links) | Lead organizer |
| T + 24 to 48 h | One educational thread OR blog-style notes | Whoever writes fastest |

If you miss the window, detail evaporates. “Great energy” is not a recap.

### Repurpose ladder (pick two per event)

```text
live moment --> clip --> thread --> newsletter blurb --> Builder Hub deep link
```

Examples:

- **Clip:** 45 seconds of a demo that names the repo and chain plainly.
- **Thread:** five tweets: problem, what we tried, link to slides, link to [Custom L1s](https://build.avax.network/academy/avalanche-l1/permissionless-l1s) or another relevant course, RSVP for next date.
- **Deep link:** send beginners to [Avalanche Fundamentals](https://build.avax.network/academy/avalanche-l1/avalanche-fundamentals), not a firehose of PDFs.

### Formats that age well

| Format | Good for | Light lift version |
| --- | --- | --- |
| FAQ from live Q&A | Builders who could not attend | Paste questions into Notion, answer async |
| Speaker one-pager | Partners evaluating you | PDF with bio, talk title, three links |
| Workshop repo tag | Technical series | `v1.0-event-date` in GitHub |
| Photo set with captions | Sponsors and university clubs | Five captioned images beats fifty silent shots |

### Consent and safety

- Get clear **on-camera consent** in venues where faces are visible. A simple verbal norm at intros helps.
- Do not post sensitive wallet setups, seed phrases, or attendee mistakes for laughs.
- When in doubt, blur screens and **ask** before tagging handles in failure stories.

### Questions to think through

1. **Stack:** Which two channels does your audience actually read within 48 hours?
2. **Owner:** Who is named on the runbook for clip pick + thread publish?
3. **Bridge:** Which single Builder Hub course will you staple to each recap for the next quarter?
4. **Proof:** What metric will you attach to content (link clicks, course starts, Discord joins), even if it is imperfect?
