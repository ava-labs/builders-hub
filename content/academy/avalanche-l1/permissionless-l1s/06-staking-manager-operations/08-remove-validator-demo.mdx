This demo walks you through removing a validator from your Permissionless L1, returning your staked tokens and claiming any earned rewards. The tool handles all phases including the optional delegation fee claim:

1. **Select** - Choose the validator to remove
2. **Initiate** - Start the removal process (with optional uptime proof)
3. **P-Chain** - Sign and submit the weight update to the P-Chain
4. **Complete** - Finalize the removal and receive your stake + rewards
5. **Claim Fees** (Optional) - Claim any accumulated delegation fees

<Callout type="warning">
You can only remove a validator after its **minimum stake duration** has elapsed. Check the time remaining in the [PoS Validator Set query](/academy/permissionless-l1s/06-staking-manager-operations/02-query-pos-validator-set).
</Callout>
