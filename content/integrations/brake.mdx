## Overview

Brake is a stablecoin infrastructure platform that enables enterprises to issue, manage, and integrate digital dollars into their blockchain applications. With a focus on reliability and ease of integration, Brake provides the tools and services needed for businesses to leverage stablecoin technology without the complexity of building infrastructure from scratch.

## Features

- **Stablecoin Issuance**: Infrastructure for creating and managing backed stablecoins
- **Reserve Management**: Transparent reserve backing and management systems
- **API Integration**: Developer-friendly APIs for seamless integration
- **Compliance Support**: Tools for regulatory compliance and reporting
- **Multi-Chain Deployment**: Support for multiple blockchain networks including Avalanche
- **Enterprise Support**: Dedicated support for institutional clients

## Getting Started

To use Brake's stablecoin services:

1. **Explore Platform**: Visit the [Brake website](https://www.brake.fi/) to learn about available services
