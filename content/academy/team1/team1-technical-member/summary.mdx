## Summary & Next Steps

To summarize:

- **Everyone in Team1** should aim to understand:
  - Section 1 – Introduction to Blockchain
  - Section 2 – Introduction to Avalanche and its Ecosystem
- **Technical Team1 members** should additionally be comfortable with **at least three** of:
  - Section 3 – Introduction to Solidity
  - Section 4 – ERC Standards
  - Section 5 – Frontend Integration
  - Section 6 – Custom L1s

Use this course as:

- A **roadmap** to choose which technical areas to focus on first.
- A **self‑assessment tool**: if you can confidently answer the questions listed in a section and demonstrate working examples (projects, workshops, bootcamps), you’re on track.
- A **bridge to Builder Hub and Team1 resources**, especially:
  - Builder Hub Academy tracks for Blockchain, Avalanche L1s, and token / L1 customization.[`https://build.avax.network/academy`](https://build.avax.network/academy)
  - Team1 Bootcamps like the Latam Bootcamp by David and Freddy for intensive, mentor‑led training.[`https://team1-latam-bootcamp.gitbook.io/team1-docs`](https://team1-latam-bootcamp.gitbook.io/team1-docs)

As you progress, share your learnings and projects in Team1 channels and consider helping run technical workshops, so the next generation of Technical Team1 members can follow your path.
