## Overview

KKR is a global investment firm managing alternative asset classes including private equity, credit, and real assets. Through tokenized products like the Health Care Growth Fund, KKR offers qualified investors access to institutional-grade private market investments on blockchain, combining its investment expertise with the efficiency and transparency of distributed ledger technology.
