## Welcome to Organizing Your First Team1 Event

This course is a practical guide to taking an event from **idea → proposal → preparation → execution → follow-up**.

You’ll see this content align with:

- Official Team1 program expectations and event guidance.[`https://aadao.gitbook.io/avalanche-team1-manual`](https://aadao.gitbook.io/avalanche-team1-manual) [`https://avalancheteam1.notion.site/Team1-Member-Onboarding-269688ec20c4808fb3ece079485f0441`](https://avalancheteam1.notion.site/Team1-Member-Onboarding-269688ec20c4808fb3ece079485f0441)
- Builder Hub’s event-planning best practices for community building.[`https://build.avax.network/academy/entrepreneur/web3-community-architect/07-community-building/09-event-planning-for-community`](https://build.avax.network/academy/entrepreneur/web3-community-architect/07-community-building/09-event-planning-for-community)

## Course Modules

This course is structured into five focused modules plus a short summary:

1. **Module 1 – Introduction: Why Events Matter in Team1**  
   What events do for the ecosystem and the most common Team1 event formats.
2. **Module 2 – Making a Proposal**  
   Start with your “why,” draft a one-pager, and align internally before submitting.
3. **Module 3 – Before the Event**  
   Logistics, content, promotion, safety/accessibility, and workshop requirements.
4. **Module 4 – During the Event**  
   Hosting, run-of-show execution, community care, and documentation.
5. **Module 5 – After the Event**  
   Reporting, KPIs, community follow-up, and closing the loop inside Team1.

Use the sidebar to navigate through each module, then finish with the **Summary & Next Steps** page.
