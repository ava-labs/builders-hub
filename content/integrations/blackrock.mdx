## Overview

BlackRock is the world's largest asset manager with over $10 trillion in assets under management, bringing traditional financial products to blockchain through tokenization. Through tokenized funds like BUIDL (BlackRock USD Institutional Digital Liquidity Fund), BlackRock gives institutional investors access to blockchain-native investment products that combine traditional finance compliance with on-chain efficiency and transparency.
