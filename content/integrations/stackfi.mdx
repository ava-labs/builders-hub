## Overview

Stackfi is a capital-efficient DeFi protocol built on Avalanche that enables leveraged yield farming, lending, and trading through isolated credit accounts. Each credit account is an independent smart contract, isolating risk and preventing cascade failures across the protocol. By combining the proven architecture of Gearbox V3 with Avalanche's low fees and fast finality, Stackfi delivers institutional-grade leverage tools to DeFi users.

## Features

- **Leveraged Yield Farming**: Amplify yield positions with up to 10x leverage through isolated credit accounts.
- **Lending Markets**: Supply assets across 7 lending pools (WAVAX, WETH, USDC, USDT, DAI, LINK, BTC.b) to earn dynamic yield.
- **Isolated Credit Accounts**: Each account is a separate smart contract, containing risk and preventing cascade liquidations.
- **Multi-Collateral Support**: Borrow against multiple token types simultaneously, including Aave V3 yield-bearing aTokens.
- **Integrated Trading**: Execute leveraged trades directly within credit accounts via 1inch swap routing.
- **Real-Time Health Monitoring**: Track health factors, P&L, and liquidation risk with visual indicators.
- **Chainlink Price Feeds**: Reliable oracle infrastructure for accurate asset pricing.

## Getting Started

To begin using Stackfi:

1. **Access Platform**: Visit [Stackfi](https://stackfi.net/).
2. **Connect Wallet**: Link your Web3 wallet with AVAX for gas fees.
3. **Supply Assets**: Deposit supported assets into lending pools to earn interest.
4. **Open a Credit Account**: Borrow against your collateral with configurable leverage.
5. **Manage Positions**: Monitor health factors and adjust collateral or debt as needed.
