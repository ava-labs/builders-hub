This demo walks you through delegating native tokens to an active validator on your Permissionless L1. The tool handles all three phases:

1. **Select** - Choose an active validator to delegate to
2. **Initiate** - Lock your native tokens and start the delegation
3. **P-Chain** - Sign and submit the weight update to the P-Chain
4. **Complete** - Finalize the delegation to activate it
