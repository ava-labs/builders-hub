This demo walks you through removing a delegation from a validator on your Permissionless L1, returning your delegated tokens and claiming earned rewards (minus the delegation fee). The tool handles all three phases:

1. **Select** - Choose the delegation to remove (your active delegations are listed)
2. **Initiate** - Start the removal process (with optional uptime proof)
3. **P-Chain** - Sign and submit the weight update to the P-Chain
4. **Complete** - Finalize the removal and receive your tokens + rewards

<Callout type="warning">
You can only remove a delegation after the validator's **minimum stake duration** has elapsed. Check the time remaining in the [PoS Validator Set query](/academy/permissionless-l1s/06-staking-manager-operations/02-query-pos-validator-set) under the "My Delegations" tab.
</Callout>
