## Summary and next steps

In this course, you learned how to move from idea through proposal, preparation, execution, and follow-up for a Team1 event..

Your immediate next steps are to:

- Identify the **type of event** you want to run (meetup, workshop, university session, online event, hackathon) based on local needs and your strengths.
- Complete the **[official event proposal form](https://avalancheteam1.notion.site/215688ec20c48074bb59c0212dbd74d0)** with clear goals, audience, and budget.
- After the event, submit the **[event recap form](https://avalancheteam1.notion.site/25e688ec20c48064a093dcf7c613c36b)** within **48 hours**.
- Use the checklists in this course alongside any other official documentation for planning and reporting.
