Verify that your Staking Manager is properly configured and ready to accept validators and delegators.

## What to Verify

Check the following configuration parameters:
- Minimum and maximum stake amounts
- Stake duration requirements
- Delegation fee settings
- Reward calculator address
- Native Minter permissions
- Reward Manager ownership
