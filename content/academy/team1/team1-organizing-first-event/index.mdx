## Welcome to Organizing Your First Team1 Event

This course is a practical guide to taking an event from **idea to proposal to preparation to execution to follow-up**.

You will see this content align with:

- Official Team1 program expectations and event guidance ([Team1 Manual](https://aadao.gitbook.io/avalanche-team1-manual), [member onboarding](https://avalancheteam1.notion.site/Team1-Member-Onboarding-269688ec20c4808fb3ece079485f0441)).
- Builder Hub’s [Event planning for community building](https://build.avax.network/academy/entrepreneur/web3-community-architect/07-community-building/09-event-planning-for-community) lesson.

---

## Course modules

This course is structured into five focused modules plus a short summary:

1. **Module 1: Why events matter in Team1**  
   What events do for the ecosystem and the most common Team1 event formats.
2. **Module 2: Making a proposal**  
   Start with your “why,” align internally, then submit through the **official Team1 event proposal form** (Notion), including realistic budget lines for pre-approval.
3. **Module 3: Before the event**  
   Logistics, content, promotion, safety and accessibility, and workshop requirements.
4. **Module 4: During the event**  
   Hosting, run-of-show execution, community care, and documentation.
5. **Module 5: After the event**  
   Official recap form, Drive-based success and expense reports, KPIs, community follow-up, and closing the loop inside Team1.

Use the sidebar to navigate through each module, then finish with the **Summary and next steps** page.
