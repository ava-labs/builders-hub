## Overview

Franklin Templeton is a global investment management firm with over $1.5 trillion in assets under management. Through tokenized funds like BENJI (Franklin OnChain U.S. Government Money Fund), Franklin Templeton offers investment products on blockchain, combining traditional fund management with the efficiency and transparency of distributed ledger technology.
