## Overview

Libeara, in partnership with Wellington Management, tokenizes institutional investment products on blockchain. Through offerings like ULTRA, Libeara brings Wellington Management's asset management expertise to the blockchain ecosystem, giving investors access to institutional investment strategies through tokenized products.
