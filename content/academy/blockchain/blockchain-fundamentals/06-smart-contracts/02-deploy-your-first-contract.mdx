## Your First Smart Contract on Avalanche

In this challenge, you will deploy a smart contract to the **Avalanche Fuji Testnet**.  
When you're done, you will submit your contract address and earn your **first badge** ✨  
(coming soon!)


### Step 1 — Get Test AVAX

To deploy, you need test AVAX on Fuji.

- [Request Test AVAX](https://faucet.avax.network/)


### Step 2 — Deploy Using Remix

- Open Remix:  
https://remix.ethereum.org

- Create a new file and paste the contract:

```solidity
// SPDX-License-Identifier: MIT
pragma solidity ^0.8.20;

contract HelloAvalanche {
    string public greeting = "Hello, Avalanche!";
}
```

- Click **Compile**

- Click **Deploy**
- Connect MetaMask
- Select **Avalanche Fuji**
- Confirm the transaction

 After deployment, copy your **contract address**  
Under “Deployed Contracts”

### Step 3 — Submit Your Contract Address

To receive your Builder badge
**Submission link coming soon**

> A validator will check your deployment.

### What You Accomplished

You just:
- Deployed a smart contract to a real blockchain
- Interacted with MetaMask + Remix
- Published a public contract address

You are now officially a **blockchain builder**
