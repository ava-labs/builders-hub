## Overview

Bilira is a regulated stablecoin issuer providing TRYB, a Turkish Lira-backed stablecoin for users and businesses in Turkey and globally. With regulatory compliance and transparent reserve management, Bilira facilitates payments, remittances, and digital asset transactions denominated in Turkish Lira.
