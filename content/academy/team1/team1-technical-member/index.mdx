## Welcome to Technical Team1

Team1 is described by Avalanche as “a new global network of builders, developers, creatives, gamers and community members who grow Avalanche,” with members organizing workshops, hackathons, and other technical activities around the world.[`https://www.avax.network/ambassador`](https://www.avax.network/ambassador)  
Within that network, **Technical Team1 members** are the people who can confidently explain blockchain and Avalanche, build and deploy smart contracts, integrate contracts with frontends, and even work with custom Avalanche L1s, as reflected in the internal **Technical Knowledge** checklist and the Team1 Manual’s expectations for technical contributors.[`https://aadao.gitbook.io/avalanche-team1-manual`](https://aadao.gitbook.io/avalanche-team1-manual)

This course is **not** a full programming class. Instead, it:

- Maps the **knowledge areas** from the `TechnicalKnowledgeTeam1` document into a clear path.
- Points you to **concrete learning resources** (especially Builder Hub and Team1 Bootcamps).
- Gives you **self‑check questions** so you know when you’re ready to act as a Technical Team1 member.

The content is organized into:

- **Foundations (Sections 1–2)** – knowledge **every** Team1 member should have.
- **Technical specialization (Sections 3–6)** – knowledge areas that define a Technical Team1 profile.

## Course Modules

This course is structured into six focused sections plus a short summary:

1. **Section 1 – Introduction to Blockchain**  
   Blockchain fundamentals every Team1 member should know.
2. **Section 2 – Introduction to Avalanche and Its Ecosystem**  
   How Avalanche works, how it differs from other networks, and where L1s fit.
3. **Section 3 – Introduction to Solidity**  
   Core Solidity and smart contract concepts for Avalanche L1s.
4. **Section 4 – ERC Standards (ERC20, ERC721, ERC1155)**  
   Token and NFT standards and how to use them on Avalanche.
5. **Section 5 – Frontend Integration**  
   Connecting smart contracts to modern frontends (Next.js, wagmi, WalletConnect).
6. **Section 6 – Custom L1s on Avalanche**  
   Concepts, roles, and configuration for launching and operating Avalanche L1s.

Use the sidebar to navigate through each section, then finish with the **Summary & Next Steps** page.
